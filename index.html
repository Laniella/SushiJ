Sushi in Bellevue, WA - Sushi J - located in Crossroads Mall

Located in heart of residential Bellevue in Crossroads Mall food court, our sushi is a secret find. Delicious, affordable and easy for dine in in the food court, delivery, takeout or catering. You’ll discover a slight surprise - check out our reviews - of how good our sushi really is. Sometimes one might think a sushi takeout restaurant located in a Mall might not be as good as other sushi restaurants. We think you’ll be surprised. Check out our reviews and photos online to get a better idea of how good Sushi J really is. It’s your new, secret find.

Freshly prepared Sushi everyday

Our fish is sourced and freshly prepared daily. Check out our sushi menu to see our sushi, rolls, poke bowls and seared sushi options. Try our poke or fan-favorite spicy seared salmon to satisfy your sushi cravings.
